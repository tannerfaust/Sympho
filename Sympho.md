# Sympho

Concept: a platform that allow people conveniently plan their learning jorneys, topics, content and materials needed to really win. The idea is to adress a list of problems that a lot of ambitious people encounter all the time, like having a hard time managing study materials, topics, researches for a specfific fields or things, and many more. Demystifying how to learn topic, generation the larning plans and helping to find free content and courses

#### Problems that Sympho addresses:

- Knowledge gaps keep popping up - Hard time keeping track of things to learn
- No way to priorittise learning
- No clear learning path. - people don;t really understand the logical order ofhe things that want to learn and therefore feel frastrated and dumb.
- Learning plans die quickly
- Unclear prerequisites
- Forgetting what they’ve learned - no system to help keep trach of progress or review key concetps over time
- No visibility into their overall learning journeys
- No research managment platform for the specific projects
